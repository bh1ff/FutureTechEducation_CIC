Frequently Asked Questions

Robotics Curriculum

  • What courses are included in your robotics curriculum?
    Our robotics curriculum includes six courses designed to engage and educate students in the exciting field of robotics. These courses cover a wide range of topics, from the basics of electrical components and coding principles to advanced concepts such as AI systems and IoT. Each course is carefully crafted to provide a progressive learning experience and equip students with the skills they need for the future.
  • How long does it take to complete the robotics curriculum?
    The duration of the robotics curriculum depends on the learning pace of the students. On average, it takes approximately 120 hours to complete all six courses. However, our curriculum is flexible and can be adapted to fit within your school's schedule and needs.
  • Can your curriculum be integrated into our existing STEM program?
    Absolutely! Our curriculum is designed to seamlessly integrate into any existing STEM program. We understand that every school has its unique approach to education, and our curriculum is flexible enough to complement and enhance your current curriculum. Our team of experts will work closely with you to ensure a smooth integration that aligns with your school's goals and objectives.

Training and Support

  • What kind of training and support do you provide?
    We provide comprehensive training and support to ensure that educators and administrators feel confident and empowered to implement our robotics curriculum effectively. Our team of dedicated professionals will provide training sessions, resources, and ongoing support to ensure a smooth implementation process. We are committed to equipping educators with the knowledge and tools they need to inspire and engage students in the world of robotics.
  • Do you offer professional development workshops?
    Yes, we offer professional development workshops for educators interested in enhancing their robotics teaching skills. These workshops cover pedagogical strategies, technical knowledge, and hands-on training to equip educators with the tools they need to create an engaging and immersive learning experience. Our workshops are designed to foster a community of passionate educators who are dedicated to providing the best robotics education to their students.

Pricing and Availability

  • What is the pricing structure for your robotics curriculum?
    The pricing structure for our robotics curriculum depends on various factors such as the number of licenses required and the level of support needed. We offer competitive pricing packages tailored to meet the specific needs of your school or institution. Please reach out to our team for more information and a detailed pricing quote.
  • Is the robotics curriculum available nationally?
    Yes, our robotics curriculum is available nation wide. We are dedicated to bringing the power of STEM education and robotics to students all over the UK. Whether you are in the West Midlands or any other part of the UK, we are ready to support and collaborate with you to enhance your students' learning experience.
